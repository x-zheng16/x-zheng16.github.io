I am a final-year Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Cong Wang and working closely with Dr. Xingjun Ma and Prof. Chao Shen. My research interests lie in the intersection of reinforcement learning and trustworthy AI. Particularly, I am interested in developing efficient reinforcement learning algorithms for robot learning, adversarial policy learning, and AI red teaming.

I received my Master degree from the Department of Automation, Tsinghua University in 2019, supervised by Prof. Tao Zhang, and obtained my Bachelor degree from Shen Yuan Honors College (formerly School of Advanced Engineering), Beihang University in 2016.
