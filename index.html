I am currently a final-year Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Cong Wang. I also work in close collaboration with Prof. Xingjun Ma from Fudan University and Prof. Chao Shen from Xi’an Jiaotong University. My primary research focus is on the intersection of reinforcement learning and trustworthy AI. Specifically, I am passionate about designing efficient reinforcement learning algorithms for applications such as robot learning, adversarial policy learning, and AI red teaming.

I received my Master’s degree in 2019 from the Department of Automation at Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Tao Zhang. Prior to that, I obtained my Bachelor’s degree in 2016 from the Honors College (formerly School of Advanced Engineering) at Beihang University.
